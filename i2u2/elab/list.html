List of e-Labs Available to Students

Physics Labs

Cosmic Ray e-Lab Production Version with Grid Execution - High school students investigate data from a cosmic ray detector array. (It is not necessary to have a detector to participate.) Possible student investigations include:

  • Muon Lifetime
  • Diurnal changes in flux
  • Effects of shielding
  • High-energy showers
  • Altitude effects

CMS Test Beam e-Lab (Alpha Version) - High school students analyze CMS test beam data in an online graphical ROOT environment.

  • Shower Depth
  • Lateral Shower Size
  • Beam Purity
  • Detector Resolution

LIGO e-Lab (Beta version) - High school and middle school students investigate seismic behavior with data from LIGO (Laser Interferometer Gravitational-wave Observatory).

  • Earthquake Studies
  • Frequency Band Studies
  • Microseismic Studies
  • Studies of Human-induced Seismic Activity
