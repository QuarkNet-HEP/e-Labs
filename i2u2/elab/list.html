List of e-Labs Available to Students

Physics Labs

Cosmic Ray e-Lab - High school students investigate data from a cosmic ray detector array. (It is not necessary to have a detector to participate.) Possible student investigations include:

  • Muon Lifetime
  • Diurnal changes in flux
  • Effects of shielding
  • High-energy showers
  • Altitude effects

LIGO e-Lab - High school and middle school students investigate seismic behavior with data from LIGO (Laser Interferometer Gravitational-wave Observatory).

  • Earthquake Studies
  • Frequency Band Studies
  • Microseismic Studies
  • Studies of Human-induced Seismic Activity

CMS e-Lab - High school students analyze CMS simulated and run data in an online graphical environment.

  • Calibration Study
