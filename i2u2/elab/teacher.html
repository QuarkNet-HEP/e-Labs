Teacher Registration

For your students to use e-Labs, you need to register as a teacher. Fill out this Email Form.

Once you receive your account for the e-Lab you will be able to register your students, keep track of their progress using logbooks, pretests and posttests, and see the products of their research. Please read the teacher pages accessible from the project page for each e-Lab.
