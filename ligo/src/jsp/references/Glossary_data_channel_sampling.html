Sampling

For DMT channels, you will see rms as the only option in this field. For PEM channels you will see rms and mean. rms stands for "Root Mean Square" and is calculated by squaring each individual value in the data set, summing all the squared values, dividing the sum by the number of values and taking the square root.
Beware of choosing mean when looking at PEM channels. The mean sometimes will cause the (+) and (-) swings in the seismometer signals to cancel out, leading the investigator to conclude that the seismic signal was smaller than was actually the case.
