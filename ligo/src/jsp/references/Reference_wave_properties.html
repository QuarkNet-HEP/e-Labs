Milestone: Describe basic wave properties.

Research proposals center on a single, well-designed research question that can be answered by collecting and analyzing data.

To use LIGO's seismic data, you need to understand wave properties. What interesting measurements can we make? You may already know about familiar waves—water waves, light waves or sound waves. What about seismic waves, those that result from the ground shaking?

Briefly describe these properties of seismic waves in your logbook: speed, wavelength, frequency, amplitude and arrival time.
