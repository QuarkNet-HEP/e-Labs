Milestone: Describe basic wave properties.

Background information is part of any research proposal. Scientists create new knowledge by building on basic science principles.

To use LIGO's seismic data, you need to understand wave properties. What interesting wave measurements can you make? You may already know about familiar waves—water waves, light waves or sound waves. What about seismic waves, those that result from the ground shaking?

Briefly describe these properties of seismic waves in your logbook: speed, wavelength, frequency, amplitude and arrival time.
