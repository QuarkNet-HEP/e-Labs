Milestone: Identify locations of LIGO seismometers.

A research project must be designed around the equipment.

LIGO Hanford Observatory runs five seismometers in different locations along the beam tubes. You will need to know these locations and how they are labeled to analyze the data.

Devise a scheme in your logbook to keep track of the seismometer labels.
