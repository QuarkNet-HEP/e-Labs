Milestone: Examine and become familiar with LIGO seismic data.

Research proposals center on a single, well-designed research question that can be answered by collecting and analyzing data.

The LIGO observatories each run six seismometers in different locations along the beam tubes. You need to know these locations and how they are labeled to analyze the data.

Bluestone is our LIGO analysis tool. Take some time to practice making plots with Bluestone.

Use the navigation bar to go to Bluestone. Click on Practice Plots on the right.

As you make more plots, look for interesting features of the data. You can save plots in your logbook. Eventually you'll need to pick a feature as the focus of your research.

In your logbook insert the two most interesting plots that you saved. Add some comments about what you find interesting in your plots and what might be a research question you could investigate.
