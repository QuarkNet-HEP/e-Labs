Milestone: Improve your answer

The results of your test may have indicated that your hypothesis needs to be changed. Keep repeating the cycle of analysis-hypothesis-test-improve until you can produce clear and convincing evidence that your answer is strong. You might go through the cyle once or many times. Your goal is to create the strongest possible explanation of the event, trend or pattern that you saw in your data when you made your research question.
