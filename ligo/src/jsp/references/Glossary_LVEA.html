Laser and Vacuum Equipment Area (LVEA)

The Laser and Vacuum Equipment Area (LVEA) is the area where the beam tubes meet in a LIGO detector. LIGO's interferometers have an "L" shape. The LVEA is the interior of the building at the vertex of the L. The LVEA is a laser safety area that requires special precautions and clean room clothing for entry. There is a seismometer in each LIGO LVEA that is part of the I2U2 data network.
