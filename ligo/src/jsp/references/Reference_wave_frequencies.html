Milestone: Learn how to estimate wave frequencies.

LIGO scientists study plots of the physics data looking for the gravitational waves that no one has ever seen.

You will be studying a particular feature in the LIGO seismic data to determine the source. Was it caused by an earthquake, traffic driving by or maybe a rabbit hopping?

LIGO separates seismic data into frequency bands. You will need to be able to interpret the frequency labels on the plots. For example, you might see the label Seismic x, Bandwidth filtered, 0.1 - 0.3 Hz where 0.1-0.3 Hz is the frequency band for the plot.

Get a better sense of different frequencies. Study the examples and then practice estimating the frequencies on new plots.
