Milestone: Make a research question and plan.

The research question organizes all aspects of the project. A clear research question determines how scientists do their research and what resources they will need.

What interesting events or features have you seen in the LIGO data that you have studied so far? Think of the processes that might have caused the event or feature. But think carefully! You may be able to rule out a number of explanations that don't match the nature of the event.

What questions do you have about ground vibrations and seismic waves? What will you need to look for in additional plots to answer your research question?

Remember, a good research question must allow you to pursue the answer through additional research.
