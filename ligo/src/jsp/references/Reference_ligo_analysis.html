Milestone: Analyze data to answer your research question

To meet this milestone, you must develop an answer (a hypothesis) for your research question. You've identified a seismic event, trend or pattern on one or more plots that you have made. Now you are saying "I think that this event, trend or pattern is caused by ______ ." Is your idea a good one? You'll find out as you tackle the next milestone!
