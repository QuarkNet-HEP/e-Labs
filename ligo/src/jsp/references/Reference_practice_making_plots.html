Milestone: Learn how to read LIGO seismic data.

Research proposals center on a single, well-designed research question that can be answered by collecting and analyzing data.

Get to know the seismic data by making a number of different practice plots.

How did the ground shake at LIGO on your birthday?
What does the data look like for a whole week?
What does the data look like on the same day for several years?

As you make more plots, you'll start to recognize interesting features of the data. Eventually you'll need to pick a feature as the focus of your research.

The tutorial will show you how to use Bluestone to create a plot.

Keep track of the interesting plots you make in your logbook.
