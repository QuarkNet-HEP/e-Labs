Milestone: Practice making plots of LIGO data

You can use LIGO's Bluestone software to plot LIGO seismic data. Before you establish a research question and research plan, practice by making a number of different seismic plots. These will help you get a feel for the appearance of the data. You will also learn how to choose a seismometer and a data channel. As you make more plots, you'll start to recognize interesting features of the data. Eventually you'll need to pick a feature and make it the focus of your research question and research plan. Use the Bluestone tutorial to help you learn how to plot data.
