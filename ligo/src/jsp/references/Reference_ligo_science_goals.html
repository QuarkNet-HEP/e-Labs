Milestone: Describe the science goals of LIGO.

Scientific research involves observing nature to learn new facts or testing the fit between new ideas and known facts. Scientists must write proposals to get financial support for their research projects.

You can be a LIGO student researcher diagnosing "seismic noise." First, you must understand LIGO's scientific mission.

As you begin your diagnosis, you should get used to keeping daily notes in your online e-Lab logbook.

In your logbook, write the major question that LIGO scientists want to answer.
