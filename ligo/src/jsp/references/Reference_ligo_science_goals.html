Milestone: Describe the science goals of LIGO.

Scientific research involves observing nature to learn new facts or testing the fit between new ideas and known facts. Scientists must write proposals to get financial support for their reseach projects.

You can be a LIGO student researcher diagnosing "seismic noise." First, you must understand LIGO's mission.

As you begin your research, you should get used to keeping daily notes in a logbook. Check to see if you should be using our e-Logbook.

In your logbook, write the major question that LIGO scientists want to answer. Can you dig deeper and come up with follow-on questions?
