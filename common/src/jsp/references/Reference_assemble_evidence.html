Milestone: Assemble evidence for your results.

Scientists must convince each other that their conclusions make sense. Data, analysis and interpretation are tools that show the research details and defend results.

Look at the plots you saved using the View Plots submenu under Data. Also check your logbook for plots that support or do not support your ideas. Based on your notes, list plots that you think should appear in your published results.
