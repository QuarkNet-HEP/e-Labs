Milestone: Choose appropriate data from the data set.

Scientists need data to answer their research questions.

First you need to choose your data. But which data? Your research question will guide you. Exploring the available data may lead you to refine or revise your study.You choose data in two places:

  • Select the data sets- Test Beam, Simulations, etc.
  • Apply the data filters with the drag and drop in the data selection window.

Is there enough data, and of the right kind, to support your inquiry? Use OGRE to find out!

Keep track of data appropriate for your inquiry in your logbook.
