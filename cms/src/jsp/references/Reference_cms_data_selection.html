Milestone: Select appropriate data from the data set.

Scientists need data to answer their research questions.

First you need to choose your data. But which data? Your research question will guide you. Exploring the available data may lead you to refine or revise your study.

CMS data comes in runs of varying length and consists of events. Physicists filter the data so they can look at specific event types (e.g., events with two muons).

Choose the event type you are studying. Run Selection and Advanced Data Selection allows you to display and refine your selection.

Click on "Calibration" under "Data" in the navigation bar.
