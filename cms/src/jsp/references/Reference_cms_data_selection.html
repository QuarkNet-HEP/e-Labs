Milestone: Choose appropriate data from the data set.

Data can be choosen in OGRE either by particle type or by individual data run. But which data should you choose? Your choice of research question will guide you, but exploring the available data may lead you to refine or revise your research question. Is there enough data, and of the right kinds, to support your inquiry? Use OGRE to find out!
