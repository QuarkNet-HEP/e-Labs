Milestone: Choose appropriate data from the data set.

Scientists need data to answer their research questions.

You can choose data in OGRE either by particle type or by individual data run. But which data should you choose? Your choice of study will guide you. Exploring the available data may lead you to refine or revise your study.

Is there enough data, and of the right kind, to support your inquiry? Use OGRE to find out!

Keep track of data appropriate for your inquiry in your logbook.
