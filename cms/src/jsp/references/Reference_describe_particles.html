Milestone: Describe the particles interacting in the CMS detector.

Scientists often explain the behavior of complex systems in terms of the interactions among less complex parts.

What cast of particle characters do we need for CMS investigations? Some particles are fundamental; others are made of simpler parts. Protons interact in the primaray collision; other interactions take place in the time between the primary collision and direct detection in CMS.
