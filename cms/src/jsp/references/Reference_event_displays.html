Milestone: : Interpret basic event displays.

Scientists often represent their data graphically . .

You have already learned about particles that interact with the CMS detector. Physicists call the interaction an event. How do these events appear in data displays?

2-D single event displays are a great way to take a look. Click on this image to see the CMS Z decay.

Watch a screencast of clean Z decays at lower energy from an earlier CERN experiment.

With greater collision energies at the LHC, the Z decay will not be the only collision products in the event nor will the decay products seem to be back-to-back.
