Milestone: Briefly describe the CMS test beam setup.

A research project must be designed around capabilities of the equipment. Scientists push the limit of their instruments with their questions. Sometimes scientists must wait for new technologies to answer their research questions.

The 14,000 ton CMS detector is the product of some 2,000 scientists and engineers from 40 countries. You, too, can work with CMS, starting with the two detector components set up in the test beam.
