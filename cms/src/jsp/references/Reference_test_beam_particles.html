Milestone: Describe the particles used in the test beam.

Scientific research involves observing nature to learn new facts or test applications of theories to known facts.

Particle physicists study fundamental particles and the interactions among them. The Standard Model is the theoretical framework that describes these particles. The Large Hadron Collider, LHC, is the CERN accelerator that sends a beam of particles to the Compact Muon Solenoid detector, CMS.
