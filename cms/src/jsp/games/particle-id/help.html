Help on Identifying Events

The Z particle can decay in four different ways. Each creates a pair of particles, muons, electrons, taus and quarks. The two quarks quickly decay into two sprays of particles called jets. These pairs have the prefix "Di" for "two". Three decay modes are shown in these events. The Ditau is excluded because it is harder to identify with the event displays provided here.
