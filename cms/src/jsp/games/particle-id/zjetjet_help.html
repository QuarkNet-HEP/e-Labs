Dijet ( Z → jet jet)

A jet is a spray of particles represented by a yellow cone coming out of the center with lots of yellow tracks. This event has two jets ("Dijet"). The yellow line indicates the angle of the center of the jet. Also notice the red and blue color bars coming from the green ring indicating the presence of both electrons and hadrons. The longer the bar, the greater the energy of the particles. None of the outer muon chambers are lit up.
