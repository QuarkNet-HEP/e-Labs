Ditau ( Z → τ τ)

The decay of a Z to two tau particles, a ditau, is sometimes hard to distinguish from the decay of a Z to two electrons, a dielectron. Other information about the event not shown in these event displays provides more clues to help identify the dimuon. Taus leave tracks in the center tracker and some energy in the electromagnetic and hadronic calorimeters which show up as red and blue bars extending from the green ring.
