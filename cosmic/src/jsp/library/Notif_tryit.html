Step-by-Step Instructions: Notifications

First, we shall log in as a teacher and send a notification to 'undergrads'.
Step 1: Click on the Notifications icon.
Step 2: a.) See all notifications sent to you.
b.) If you are a teacher, you can send notifications.
Step 3: Send notification (for teachers only):
a.) Choose group you wish to send notifications to.
b.) Alternatively, send notification to all your groups.
c.) Type your message.
d.) If you want your notification to expire, click the box, and choose how long before it expires.
e.) Click send.
Second, we shall log in as a student and view the notification.
Step 4: Click on the Notifications icon.
Step 5: a.) A click on 'New' will mark the notification as 'Read'.
b.) Click 'See all notifications' to view the ones sent to you.
Step 6: View notifications sent to you.
