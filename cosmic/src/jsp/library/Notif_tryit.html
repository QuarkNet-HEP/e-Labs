Step-by-Step Instructions: Notifications

Step 1: After logging in, click on the Notifications icon.
Step 2: a.) See all notifications sent to you.
b.) If you are a teacher, you can send notifications.
Step 3: For teachers only. a.) Choose group you wish to send notifications to.
b.) Alternatively, send notification to all your groups.
c.) Type your message.
d.) If you want your notification to expire, click the box, and choose how long before it expires.
e.) Click send.
