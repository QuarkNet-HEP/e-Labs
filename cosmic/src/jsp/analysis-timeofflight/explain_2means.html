Time of Flight Study: 2 means

Note: There are fairly large errors (several ns) for the time of arrival of one muon, due to detector responses, compared to the time it takes a muon to traverse the distance between counters (1ns for each foot of separation). Therefore, we use the mean of the distribution of Δt's to average over all the muons. All muons have almost the same speed, so this is a good strategy.
