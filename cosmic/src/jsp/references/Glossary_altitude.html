Altitude is the vertical elevation distance measured from mean sea level. Measure altitude by:
a) Elevation contours from a topographic map: See MapServer for online topo maps.
b) Instrument measurement using a barometer: See Barometer for online instructions.
c) Finding an elevation of a nearby landmark: See Elevation for online location selection.
d) Using a survey quality GPS unit. Inexpensive hand-held units will approximate altitude to within only
100 ft.
