Milestone: Know how to collect data and upload files to our server.

You need data to answer your research questions. Some groups in the collaboration have detectors and collect data for everyone.

Scientific research is iterative. As you "figure it out," you will circle around the loop more than once to refine your analyses.

If you have a detector, read on. If not, you are ready to learn how to use the analysis tools you have selected for your research.

Use the navigation bar to find your study.

You will set up your hardware to collect data. Then you will upload data files to a central server so they are available to everyone.

Follow these steps to set up the detector, collect data and upload files.

Use the navigation bar to upload data and geometry.

Keep track of upload details in your logbook.
