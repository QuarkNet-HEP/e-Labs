DAQ: QuarkNet Data Acquisition Board - Electronic device that interprets the raw photomultiplier tube (PMT) signals and searches for patterns within them. If the signals match a user-specified pattern, the DAQ sends data to a local computer. The DAQ also interprets data from an attached GPS antenna (for timing the arrival of raw PMT signals), as well as sensors for temperature and barometric pressure. See: Electronic Simulation - shower study data aquisition. Every DAQ has its unique detector id.
