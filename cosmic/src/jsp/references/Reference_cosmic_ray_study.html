Milestone: Determine what you can study about cosmic rays.

Research proposals center on a single, well-designed research question that can be answered by collecting and analyzing data. A good question requires measurements that are neither too simple nor too complex.

  • Too simple: The answer already known and not interesting.
  • Too complex: The question is not feasible. You cannot do it with the time and equipment available.

Tips from the experts help organize your ideas.
